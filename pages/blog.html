Band Camp

 

BAND CAMP, 2022. The early morning of the summer in july we'd go outside around 7:00 am to get ready for the day of band camp.  Starting with a strech block, to going to basics, We'd be working hard every minute to make ourselves better as Marching Band members. After we do an hour of basics, We get ready to learn more and more of our years show. We do Band camp for two weeks.      

Trip to Washington DC.

            

On may 26th, We got on a bus in the morning and traveled up to DC after an 8 hour bus ride. then after that we had dinner then went to a marine barracks to watch a concer. After that we went to bed to wake up the next morning to go on our own journies with our groups around dc. then after that we had dinner, visited some memorials then went back to the hotel for bed. Then the next day we went to the WWII Memorial to lay a wrief infront of the NC Pillar. Then went to alrington National Cemetary to see the tomb of the unknown solder, then after that ventured off with our groups. After that we went to dinner, and then saw the National Memorial Day Concert. Then we went back to our hotel to go to bed for the night. We woke up the next morning to get ready for the Parade. then went to ur spot and waited. we marched in the parade then went home. We arrived at Cox Mill High around 12:30 am.                                     

Band Banquet  

on the night of may 31st 2023, we went to mcgills baptist church to celebrate the achievements of the students and band for the year. it was a very emotional event as it was also the last banquet with mr singer. lots of tears were shed, but at the end we all had a great time.
